# Day 49 - Economics vocabulary

## Lesson

Today, we'll learn some key nouns and adjectives related to economics. Remember to pay attention to the gender of each noun, as this affects how adjectives describe them and how they're used in sentences!

1.  **`اقْتِصَادٌ` (iqtiṣādun) - Economy**
    *   This is a **masculine** noun.
    *   *Pronunciation hint:* 'iq-ti-ṣaa-dun'.
    *   Example: `الِاقْتِصَادُ كَبِيرٌ.` (Al-iqtiṣādu kabeerun.) - The economy is big.

2.  **`مَالٌ` (mālun) - Money, Wealth**
    *   This is a **masculine** noun. It refers to the general concept of money or wealth. You learned its broken plural `أَمْوَالٌ` (money, funds) on Day 24. For now, focus on `مَالٌ` as the singular.
    *   *Pronunciation hint:* 'maa-lun'.
    *   Example: `الْمَالُ مُهِمٌّ.` (Al-mālu muhimun.) - Money is important.

3.  **`سُوقٌ` (sūqun) - Market**
    *   This is a **masculine** noun. You encountered this word with a Kasra (`سُوقٍ`) when we learned prepositions on Day 10, meaning "in a market." Now you know its base form!
    *   *Pronunciation hint:* 'soo-qun'.
    *   Example: `السُّوقُ جَدِيدٌ.` (As-sūqu jadeedun.) - The market is new.

4.  **`سِعْرٌ` (si'run) - Price**
    *   This is a **masculine** noun.
    *   *Pronunciation hint:* 'si'-run'.
    *   Example: `السِّعْرُ صَغِيرٌ.` (As-si'ru ṣagheerun.) - The price is small.

5.  **`بَنْكٌ` (bankun) - Bank**
    *   This is a **masculine** noun.
    *   *Pronunciation hint:* 'bank-un'.
    *   Example: `الْبَنْكُ هُنَاكَ.` (Al-banku hunāka.) - The bank is there.

6.  **`شَرِكَةٌ` (sharikatun) - Company**
    *   This is a **feminine** noun.
    *   *Pronunciation hint:* 'sha-ri-ka-tun'.
    *   Example: `الشَّرِكَةُ كَبِيرَةٌ.` (Ash-sharikatū kabeeratun.) - The company is big.

7.  **`عَمَلٌ` ('amalun) - Work, Job**
    *   This is a **masculine** noun. You know the verb `يَعْمَلُ` (he works) from Day 29. Now you have the noun for "work" or "a job."
    *   *Pronunciation hint:* 'a-ma-lun'.
    *   Example: `الْعَمَلُ مُفِيدٌ.` (Al-'amalu mufīdun.) - Work is useful.

8.  **`غَنِيٌّ` (ghaniyyun) / `غَنِيَّةٌ` (ghaniyyatun) - Rich**
    *   This is an adjective. `غَنِيٌّ` for masculine singular, `غَنِيَّةٌ` for feminine singular.
    *   *Pronunciation hint:* 'gha-niy-yun' / 'gha-niy-ya-tun'.
    *   Example: `الرَّجُلُ غَنِيٌّ.` (Ar-rajulu ghaniyyun.) - The man is rich. (`الرَّجُلُ` is 'the man' from Day 28.)
    *   Example: `الْمَرْأَةُ غَنِيَّةٌ.` (Al-mar'atu ghaniyyatun.) - The woman is rich. (`الْمَرْأَةُ` is 'the woman' from Day 28.)

9.  **`فَقِيرٌ` (faqīrun) / `فَقِيرَةٌ` (faqīratun) - Poor**
    *   This is an adjective. `فَقِيرٌ` for masculine singular, `فَقِيرَةٌ` for feminine singular.
    *   *Pronunciation hint:* 'fa-qee-run' / 'fa-qee-ra-tun'.
    *   Example: `الطَّالِبُ فَقِيرٌ.` (Aṭ-ṭālibu faqīrun.) - The student is poor.
    *   Example: `الطَّالِبَةُ فَقِيرَةٌ.` (Aṭ-ṭālibatu faqīratun.) - The student (f) is poor.

You've just learned a strong set of words to begin discussing economics! Remember to use them with their correct genders and case endings.

## Examples

Let's combine our new vocabulary with grammar and sentence structures you've learned.

1.  **The economy of the state is big.** (Idafa construct + adjective)
    *   `اقْتِصَادُ الدَّوْلَةِ كَبِيرٌ.` (Iqtiṣādu ad-dawlati kabeerun.)
        *   *Notice:* `اقْتِصَادُ` (economy of) is the first part of the Idafa, masculine, so `كَبِيرٌ` (big) is masculine.

2.  **Money is important for the people.** (Nominal sentence + preposition)
    *   `الْمَالُ مُهِمٌّ لِلشَّعْبِ.` (Al-mālu muhimun li-sh-sha'bi.)
        *   *Notice:* `لِـ` (for) is a preposition (Day 10), so `الشَّعْبِ` (the people) takes a Kasra.

3.  **The minister is in the market.** (Politics vocabulary + prepositional phrase)
    *   `الْوَزِيرُ فِي السُّوقِ.` (Al-wazīru fī as-sūqi.)
        *   *Notice:* `السُّوقِ` (the market) takes a Kasra because it follows the preposition `فِي`.

4.  **The price of the new book is small.** (Idafa + adjectives)
    *   `سِعْرُ الْكِتَابِ الْجَدِيدِ صَغِيرٌ.` (Si'ru al-kitābi al-jadeedi ṣagheerun.)
        *   *Notice:* `الْكِتَابِ الْجَدِيدِ` (of the new book) is the second noun in the Idafa, taking Kasra, and its adjective `الْجَدِيدِ` matches it. `سِعْرُ` (price of) is masculine, so `صَغِيرٌ` (small) is masculine.

5.  **I want to go to the bank.** (Nominalization with `أَنْ` + purpose clause)
    *   `أُرِيدُ أَنْ أَذْهَبَ إِلَى الْبَنْكِ.` (Ureedu an adhhaba ilā al-banki.)
        *   *Notice:* `أُرِيدُ` (I want) from Day 44. `أَنْ أَذْهَبَ` (to go) with the verb taking Fatha because of `أَنْ` (Day 38, 44). `الْبَنْكِ` (the bank) takes Kasra after `إِلَى`.

6.  **The new company is big.** (Feminine noun + matching adjectives)
    *   `الشَّرِكَةُ الْجَدِيدَةُ كَبِيرَةٌ.` (Ash-sharikatū al-jadeedatū kabeeratun.)

7.  **Work is important.** (New noun + adjective)
    *   `الْعَمَلُ مُهِمٌّ.` (Al-'amalu muhimun.)

8.  **The president is rich.** (Politics vocabulary + new adjective)
    *   `الرَّئِيسُ غَنِيٌّ.` (Ar-ra'īsu ghaniyyun.)

9.  **The minister is poor.** (Politics vocabulary + new adjective)
    *   `الْوَزِيرُ فَقِيرٌ.` (Al-wazīru faqīrun.)

10. **Indeed, the company is certainly important.** (Emphasis particles `إِنَّ` and `لَـ`)
    *   `إِنَّ الشَّرِكَةَ لَمُهِمَّةٌ.` (Inna ash-sharikatā la-muhimmatun.)
        *   *Notice:* `الشَّرِكَةَ` (the company) takes Fatha because of `إِنَّ` (Day 37). `لَمُهِمَّةٌ` (certainly important) has `لَـ` attached and is feminine to match `الشَّرِكَةُ`.

11. **He said that the market is big.** (Reported speech + `أَنَّ`)
    *   `قَالَ أَنَّ السُّوقَ كَبِيرٌ.` (Qāla anna as-sūqa kabeerun.)
        *   *Notice:* `قَالَ` (he said) from Day 39. `أَنَّ` makes `السُّوقَ` (the market) take Fatha. `كَبِيرٌ` (big) is the comment.

12. **It is probable that he works in the bank.** (Modal expression + `أَنْ` + purpose)
    *   `مِنَ الْمُحْتَمَلِ أَنْ يَعْمَلَ فِي الْبَنْكِ.` (Mina al-muḥtamali an ya'mala fī al-banki.)
        *   *Notice:* `مِنَ الْمُحْتَمَلِ أَنْ` (it is probable that) from Day 38. `يَعْمَلَ` (he works) takes Fatha because of `أَنْ`. `الْبَنْكِ` takes Kasra after `فِي`.

13. **I read news about the economy.** (News collocation + `عَنْ` + Idafa)
    *   `أَقْرَأُ أَخْبَارًا عَنِ الِاقْتِصَادِ.` (Aqra'u akhbāran 'ani al-iqtiṣādi.)
        *   *Notice:* `أَخْبَارًا` (news) is indefinite object taking Tanween Fatha. `عَنِ` (about) is a preposition (Day 48), so `الِاقْتِصَادِ` (the economy) takes Kasra.
