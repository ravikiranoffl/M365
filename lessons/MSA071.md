# Day 71 - Weekly presentation

## Lesson

Today, your goal is to prepare and deliver a short presentation in Modern Standard Arabic. This is not about learning new things, but about actively *using* what you've diligently learned.

**How to approach your weekly presentation:**

1.  **Choose a Topic:** Select a topic that allows you to use vocabulary and grammar from the past few weeks. Good options might include:
    *   The importance of the environment.
    *   Health problems and solutions.
    *   The role of education in society.
    *   A political or economic issue.
    *   A topic about technology or communication.
2.  **Structure Your Presentation:** Just like in English, a good presentation has a clear beginning, middle, and end.
    *   **Opening:** Greet your audience and introduce yourself and your topic.
    *   **Body (Main Points):** Discuss 2-3 main ideas related to your topic. Use reporting verbs, diplomatic phrasing, and persuasive language.
    *   **Examples/Elaboration:** Provide examples or further explanations for your points.
    *   **Conclusion:** Summarize your main idea and perhaps express a hope or call to action.
3.  **Draft Your Presentation:** Write out your presentation in Arabic. Focus on getting the sentences correct, using the right verb moods, case endings, and agreements.
4.  **Practice Speaking:** Read your presentation aloud. Pay attention to your pronunciation and try to speak smoothly.

Let's look at some phrases you can use for different parts of your presentation, drawing from recent lessons:

### A. Opening Your Presentation

*   **Greeting:** `مَرْحَبًا بِكُمْ.` (Marḥaban bikum.) - Welcome (to you all).
*   **Introducing Yourself:** `أَنَا مُدَرِّسٌ.` (Ana mudarrisun.) - I am a teacher. (Or state your name: `اِسْمِي مُدَرِّسٌ.` - My name is Mudarris.)
*   **Introducing Your Topic:** `مَوْضُوعُ الْيَوْمِ بِخُصُوصِ...` (Mawḍū'u al-yawmi bi-khuṣūṣi...) - Today's topic is concerning...
    *   *Example:* `مَوْضُوعُ الْيَوْمِ بِخُصُوصِ الْبِيئَةِ.` (Mawḍū'u al-yawmi bi-khuṣūṣi al-bī'ati.) - Today's topic is concerning the environment.

### B. Developing Your Main Points (Body)

*   **Stating Importance:** `الْـ... مُهِمٌّ / مُهِمَّةٌ.` (Al-... muhimun / muhimmatun.) - The... is important.
    *   *Example:* `الْبِيئَةُ مُهِمَّةٌ لِلْإِنْسَانِ.` (Al-bī'atu muhimmatun li-l-insāni.) - The environment is important for humans.
*   **Affirming a Fact (Day 64):** `أُؤَكِّدُ أَنَّ الْـ... مُهِمٌّ / مُهِمَّةٌ.` (U'akkidu anna al-... muhimun / muhimmatun.) - I affirm that the... is important.
    *   *Example:* `أُؤَكِّدُ أَنَّ صِحَّةَ الشَّعْبِ مُهِمَّةٌ.` (U'akkidu anna ṣiḥḥata ash-sha'bi muhimmatun.) - I affirm that the health of the people is important.
*   **Stating a Necessity (Day 67):** `يَجِبُ أَنْ نَفْعَلَ ...` (Yajibu an naf'ala...) - It is necessary that we do... (`نَفْعَلَ` - we do, subjunctive).
    *   *Example:* `يَجِبُ أَنْ نَحْفَظَ الْبِيئَةَ.` (Yajibu an naḥfaẓa al-bī'ata.) - It is necessary to protect the environment.
*   **Stating Clarity (Day 69):** `مِنَ الْوَاضِحِ أَنَّ...` (Mina al-wāḍiḥi anna...) - It is clear that...
    *   *Example:* `مِنَ الْوَاضِحِ أَنَّ التَّلَوُّثَ مُشْكِلَةٌ كَبِيرَةٌ.` (Mina al-wāḍiḥi anna at-talawwutha mushkilatun kabeeratun.) - It is clear that pollution is a big problem.
*   **Expressing Belief (Day 64):** `أَعْتَقِدُ أَنَّ...` (A'taqidu anna...) - I believe that...
    *   *Example:* `أَعْتَقِدُ أَنَّ الْعَمَلَ الْمُشْتَرَكَ مُفِيدٌ.` (A'taqidu anna al-'amala al-mushtaraka mufīdun.) - I believe that joint work is useful.

### C. Adding Details and Examples

*   **Connecting Cause and Effect (Day 69):** `لِذَلِكَ...` (Li-dhālika...) - Therefore...
    *   *Example:* `مُهِمٌّ أَنْ نَفْهَمَ الْمُشْكِلَةَ، لِذَلِكَ نَعْمَلُ.` (Muhimun an nafham al-mushkilata, li-dhālika na'malu.) - It is important to understand the problem; therefore, we work.
*   **Providing Examples (Day 70):** `عَلَى سَبِيلِ الْمِثَالِ،...` (Ala sabeeli al-mithāli,...) - For example,...
    *   *Example:* `هُنَاكَ مُشْكِلَاتٌ كَثِيرَةٌ، عَلَى سَبِيلِ الْمِثَالِ، تَلَوُّثُ الْمَاءِ.` (Hunāka mushkilātun katheeratun, 'alā sabeeli al-mithāli, talawwuthu al-mā'i.) - There are many problems; for example, water pollution.

### D. Concluding Your Presentation

*   **Concluding Phrase (Day 70):** `فِي الْخِتَامِ،...` (Fī al-khitāmi,...) - In conclusion,...
    *   *Example:* `فِي الْخِتَامِ، نَأْمُلُ أَنْ نَعْمَلَ يَدًا بِيَدٍ.` (Fī al-khitāmi, na'mulu an na'mala yadan bi-yadin.) - In conclusion, we hope to work hand in hand.
*   **Expressing Hope (Day 67):** `نَأْمُلُ أَنْ نَحْفَظَ الْـ...` (Na'mulu an naḥfaẓa al-...) - We hope to protect the...
    *   *Example:* `نَأْمُلُ أَنْ نَحْفَظَ بِيئَةَ الدَّوْلَةِ.` (Na'mulu an naḥfaẓa bī'ata ad-dawlati.) - We hope to protect the state's environment.

This structure provides a framework for you to build your own presentation. Don't feel pressured to use *every* single phrase, but try to use a good variety from what you've learned.

## Examples

Here is a short sample presentation on "The Importance of Protecting the Environment" using phrases you've learned.

`مَرْحَبًا بِكُمْ جَمِيعًا.` (Marḥaban bikum jamee'an.) - Welcome everyone.
`اِسْمِي مُدَرِّسٌ.` (Ismi mudarrisun.) - My name is Mudarris.
`مَوْضُوعُ الْيَوْمِ بِخُصُوصِ الْبِيئَةِ الْمُهِمَّةِ.` (Mawḍū'u al-yawmi bi-khuṣūṣi al-bī'ati al-muhimmatī.) - Today's topic is concerning the important environment.

`أُؤَكِّدُ أَنَّ الْبِيئَةَ نَظِيفَةٌ جَمِيلَةٌ.` (U'akkidu anna al-bī'ata naẓīfatun jameelatun.) - I affirm that the environment is clean and beautiful.
`مِنَ الْوَاضِحِ أَنَّ الْبِيئَةَ مُهِمَّةٌ لِصِحَّةِ الشَّعْبِ.` (Mina al-wāḍiḥi anna al-bī'ata muhimmatun li-ṣiḥḥati ash-sha'bi.) - It is clear that the environment is important for the health of the people.
`مَعَ ذَلِكَ، هُنَاكَ مُشْكِلَاتٌ كَثِيرَةٌ، عَلَى سَبِيلِ الْمِثَالِ، تَلَوُّثُ الْمَاءِ وَ الْهَوَاءِ.` (Ma'a dhālika, hunāka mushkilātun katheeratun, 'alā sabeeli al-mithāli, talawwuthu al-mā'i wa al-hawā'i.) - Nevertheless, there are many problems; for example, water and air pollution.

`لِذَلِكَ، يَجِبُ أَنْ نَعْمَلَ يَدًا بِيَدٍ لِحِفْظِ الْبِيئَةِ.` (Li-dhālika, yajibu an na'mala yadan bi-yadin li-ḥifẓi al-bī'ati.) - Therefore, it is necessary to work hand in hand to protect the environment.
`مُهِمٌّ أَنْ نَحْفَظَ الْأَشْجَارَ وَ الْمَاءَ.` (Muhimun an naḥfaẓa al-ashjāra wa al-mā'a.) - It is important to protect the trees and the water.

`فِي الْخِتَامِ، نَأْمُلُ أَنْ نَعْمَلَ جَمِيعًا لِتَحْقِيقِ بِيئَةٍ نَظِيفَةٍ.` (Fī al-khitāmi, na'mulu an na'mala jamee'an li-taḥqīqi bī'atin naẓīfatin.) - In conclusion, we hope to work all together to achieve a clean environment.
