# Day 137 - Fluency: Science reporting

## Lesson

"Fluency: Science reporting" today is all about integrated application and advanced synthesis. You already possess a strong vocabulary for discussing `عِلْمٌ` (science), `أَبْحَاثٌ` (research projects), `تَجَارِبُ` (experiments), `اكْتِشَافَاتٌ` (discoveries), `نَتَائِجُ` (results), `تَطَوُّرٌ` (development), and the `خُبَرَاءُ` (experts) in these fields. The goal now is to use these terms within even more complex sentences, connecting scientific ideas to political, economic, environmental, or cultural contexts, and employing your full range of advanced connectors, stance markers, and argumentation phrases.

Think of yourself as a seasoned analyst, capable of crafting insightful reports or discussions where you seamlessly blend scientific observations with analytical points, drawing deep connections between different aspects of a society.

To achieve this high level of fluency, remember to consistently apply and integrate:

1.  **Your `Science Reporting` Vocabulary (Days 123 & 130):**
    *   `عِلْمِيٌّ` / `عِلْمِيَّةٌ` (scientific)
    *   `أَبْحَاثٌ` (research projects, studies)
    *   `تَجْرِبَةٌ` / `تَجَارِبُ` (experiment / experiments)
    *   `اكْتِشَافٌ` / `اكْتِشَافَاتٌ` (discovery / discoveries)
    *   `يَكْتَشِفُ` (he discovers)
    *   `نَتِيجَةٌ` / `نَتَائِجُ` (result / results)
    *   `تَطَوُّرٌ` (development) / `يَتَطَوَّرُ` (he develops)
    *   `خَبِيرٌ` / `خُبَرَاءُ` (expert / experts)
    *   `يُثْبِتُ` (he proves, establishes)
    *   `يُشِيرُ إِلَى` (he indicates, points to)
    *   `الطِّبُّ` (medicine)
    *   `الْعُلَمَاءُ` (scholars)
    *   `تَمْوِيلٌ` (funding), `تِقْنِيَّاتٌ حَدِيثَةٌ` (modern technologies), `بَعْضٌ` (some), `يَعْتَمِدُ عَلَى` (he depends on).

2.  **Your `Argumentation Structures` (Days 125 & 132):** Use these to make claims, provide evidence, acknowledge contrasts, and draw conclusions.
    *   `مِنَ الْمُؤَكَّدِ أَنَّ` (It is certain that...)
    *   `مِنَ الْمَعْرُوفِ أَنَّ` (It is known that...)
    *   `لَا شَكَّ أَنَّ` (There is no doubt that...)
    *   `يَعْتَقِدُ ... أَنَّ` (... believes that...)
    *   `يَبْدُو أَنَّ` (It seems that...)
    *   `مِنَ الْوَاضِحِ أَنَّ` (It is clear that...)
    *   `لَا يُنْكَرُ أَنَّ` (It cannot be denied that...)
    *   `وَالدَّلِيلُ عَلَى ذَلِكَ أَنَّ` (And the evidence for that is that...)
    *   `لِسُوءِ الْحَظِّ` (Unfortunately...)
    *   `مِنَ الْغَرِيبِ أَنَّ` (It is strange that...)

3.  **Your `Advanced Connectors` (Days 107-108):** Ensure smooth transitions and logical relationships between ideas.
    *   `عَلَاوَةً عَلَى ذَلِكَ` (Moreover...)
    *   `مِنْ جِهَةٍ أُخْرَى` (On the other hand...)
    *   `بِالرَّغْمِ مِنْ أَنَّ` (Despite the fact that...)
    *   `فِي ضَوْءِ ذَلِكَ` (In light of that...)
    *   `وَبِنَاءً عَلَى ذَلِكَ` (And accordingly...)
    *   `خَاصَّةً وَأَنَّ` (Especially since...)
    *   `وَفِي الْوَقْتِ نَفْسِهِ` (At the same time...)
    *   `لِذَلِكَ` (Therefore...)

4.  **Impeccable `Grammatical Accuracy` (Days 118-119):** Pay meticulous attention to noun case endings, adjective agreement (especially with non-human plurals and diptotes), verb-subject agreement, Idafa constructions, `أَنَّ` vs `أَنْ`, and proper conjugation.

This session will push you to articulate nuanced and sophisticated insights about science, linking it to various aspects of society and demonstrating a high level of Arabic proficiency.

## Examples

Let's look at some examples that seamlessly integrate science reporting with other learned concepts. Carefully read each English sentence, then the Arabic translation, and pay close attention to the breakdown of how different elements are combined.

1.  **"It cannot be denied that scientific development is a key to economic progress. And the evidence for that is that modern technologies increase production. Therefore, governments should increase funding for research."**
    *   `لَا يُنْكَرُ أَنَّ التَّطَوُّرَ الْعِلْمِيَّ مِفْتَاحٌ لِلتَّقَدُّمِ الِاقْتِصَادِيِّ. وَالدَّلِيلُ عَلَى ذَلِكَ أَنَّ التِّقْنِيَّاتِ الْحَدِيثَةَ تَزِيدُ الِإنْتَاجَ. لِذَلِكَ، يَنْبَغِي أَنْ تَزِيدَ الْحُكُومَاتُ تَمْوِيلَ الْأَبْحَاثِ.`
    *   **Breakdown:**
        *   `لَا يُنْكَرُ أَنَّ` (It cannot be denied that - Day 132).
        *   `التَّطَوُّرَ الْعِلْمِيَّ مِفْتَاحٌ لِلتَّقَدُّمِ الِاقْتِصَادِيِّ` (scientific development is a key to economic progress - `التَّطَوُّرَ الْعِلْمِيَّ` (scientific development, Day 123) Accusative subject of `أَنَّ`; `مِفْتَاحٌ` (key, Day 126) Nominative predicate; `لِلتَّقَدُّمِ الِاقْتِصَادِيِّ` (to economic progress, Day 113/103)).
        *   `وَالدَّلِيلُ عَلَى ذَلِكَ أَنَّ` (And the evidence for that is that - Day 132).
        *   `التِّقْنِيَّاتِ الْحَدِيثَةَ تَزِيدُ الِإنْتَاجَ` (modern technologies increase production - `التِّقْنِيَّاتِ الْحَدِيثَةَ` (modern technologies, Day 130) Accusative subject of `أَنَّ`; `تَزِيدُ` (she increases, Day 115); `الِإنْتَاجَ` (production, Day 134) Accusative object).
        *   `لِذَلِكَ، يَنْبَغِي أَنْ تَزِيدَ الْحُكُومَاتُ تَمْوِيلَ الْأَبْحَاثِ` (Therefore, governments should increase funding for research - `لِذَلِكَ` (Day 132); `يَنْبَغِي أَنْ تَزِيدَ` (it is necessary that increase, Day 125/115 Subjunctive); `الْحُكُومَاتُ` (governments, plural of `حُكُومَةٌ` Day 101); `تَمْوِيلَ الْأَبْحَاثِ` (funding for research, Day 130/123) Idafa, Accusative object of `تَزِيدَ`).

2.  **"Despite the fact that new scientific experiments require big funding, experts believe that their results are very important for human health. On the other hand, some challenges remain regarding their application."**
    *   `بِالرَّغْمِ مِنْ أَنَّ التَّجَارِبَ الْعِلْمِيَّةَ الْجَدِيدَةَ تَتَطَلَّبُ تَمْوِيلًا كَبِيرًا، يَعْتَقِدُ الْخُبَرَاءُ أَنَّ نَتَائِجَهَا مُهِمَّةٌ جِدًّا لِصِحَّةِ الْإِنْسَانِ. مِنْ جِهَةٍ أُخْرَى، تَبْقَى بَعْضُ التَّحَدِّيَاتِ بِشَأْنِ تَطْبِيقِهَا.`
    *   **Breakdown:**
        *   `بِالرَّغْمِ مِنْ أَنَّ` (Despite the fact that - Day 107).
        *   `التَّجَارِبَ الْعِلْمِيَّةَ الْجَدِيدَةَ تَتَطَلَّبُ تَمْوِيلًا كَبِيرًا` (new scientific experiments require big funding - `التَّجَارِبَ` (experiments, Day 123) Accusative subject of `أَنَّ`; `الْعِلْمِيَّةَ الْجَدِيدَةَ` (scientific new) feminine singular adjectives for non-human plural; `تَتَطَلَّبُ` (she requires, Day 113); `تَمْوِيلًا كَبِيرًا` (big funding, Day 130/54) Accusative object).
        *   `يَعْتَقِدُ الْخُبَرَاءُ أَنَّ نَتَائِجَهَا مُهِمَّةٌ جِدًّا لِصِحَّةِ الْإِنْسَانِ` (experts believe that their results are very important for human health - `يَعْتَقِدُ الْخُبَرَاءُ أَنَّ` (Day 123/79); `نَتَائِجَهَا` (their results - `نَتَائِجُ` (results, Day 123) + `ـهَا` (Day 24)) Accusative subject of `أَنَّ`; `مُهِمَّةٌ جِدًّا` (very important, Day 54/130); `لِصِحَّةِ الْإِنْسَانِ` (for human health, Day 126/106)).
        *   `مِنْ جِهَةٍ أُخْرَى` (On the other hand - Day 108).
        *   `تَبْقَى بَعْضُ التَّحَدِّيَاتِ بِشَأْنِ تَطْبِيقِهَا` (some challenges remain regarding their application - `تَبْقَى` (she remains, Day 134); `بَعْضُ التَّحَدِّيَاتِ` (some challenges, Day 130/105) Nominative subject; `بِشَأْنِ تَطْبِيقِهَا` (regarding their application - `بِشَأْنِ` (Day 129); `تَطْبِيقِ` (application, Day 113) Genitive Masdar; `ـهَا` (Day 24)).

3.  **"Unfortunately, some scientific reports indicate that environmental pollution affects the results of agricultural research. In light of that, it cannot be denied that new solutions are needed quickly."**
    *   `لِسُوءِ الْحَظِّ، تُشِيرُ بَعْضُ التَّقَارِيرِ الْعِلْمِيَّةِ إِلَى أَنَّ التَّلَوُّثَ الْبِيئِيَّ يُؤَثِّرُ فِي نَتَائِجِ الْأَبْحَاثِ الزِّرَاعِيَّةِ. فِي ضَوْءِ ذَلِكَ، لَا يُنْكَرُ أَنَّ حُلُولًا جَدِيدَةً تُطْلَبُ بِشَكْلٍ سَرِيعٍ.`
    *   **Breakdown:**
        *   `لِسُوءِ الْحَظِّ` (Unfortunately - Day 99).
        *   `تُشِيرُ بَعْضُ التَّقَارِيرِ الْعِلْمِيَّةِ إِلَى أَنَّ` (some scientific reports indicate that - `تُشِيرُ إِلَى` (Day 123); `بَعْضُ التَّقَارِيرِ الْعِلْمِيَّةِ` (some scientific reports, Day 130/134/123) Nominative subject; `أَنَّ` (Day 119)).
        *   `التَّلَوُّثَ الْبِيئِيَّ يُؤَثِّرُ فِي نَتَائِجِ الْأَبْحَاثِ الزِّرَاعِيَّةِ` (environmental pollution affects the results of agricultural research - `التَّلَوُّثَ الْبِيئِيَّ` (environmental pollution - `التَّلَوُّثَ` (Day 124), `الْبِيئِيَّ` (environmental, adjective from `بِيئَةٌ` Day 124, new extension)) Accusative subject of `أَنَّ`; `يُؤَثِّرُ فِي` (affects, Day 122); `نَتَائِجِ الْأَبْحَاثِ الزِّرَاعِيَّةِ` (results of agricultural research - `نَتَائِجِ` (Day 123), `الْأَبْحَاثِ` (Day 123), `الزِّرَاعِيَّةِ` (agricultural, Day 134)) triple Idafa, Genitive).
        *   `فِي ضَوْءِ ذَلِكَ` (In light of that - Day 108).
        *   `لَا يُنْكَرُ أَنَّ حُلُولًا جَدِيدَةً تُطْلَبُ بِشَكْلٍ سَرِيعٍ` (it cannot be denied that new solutions are needed quickly - `لَا يُنْكَرُ أَنَّ` (Day 132); `حُلُولًا جَدِيدَةً` (new solutions - `حُلُولٌ` (solutions, Day 105), `جَدِيدَةً` (Day 54)) Accusative subject of `أَنَّ`; `تُطْلَبُ` (are needed, Day 127) feminine passive verb for non-human plural; `بِشَكْلٍ سَرِيعٍ` (quickly, Day 125)).
