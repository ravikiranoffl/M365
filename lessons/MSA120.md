# Day 120 - Monthly review

## Lesson

A monthly review is your opportunity to see how far you've come. The goal is to demonstrate your ability to integrate the various grammatical structures and specialized vocabulary you've learned. Think of it as composing an elaborate musical piece where each new phrase or chord represents a lesson you've mastered.

Today's practice will challenge you to:

1.  **Select appropriate vocabulary** from all the specialized categories learned this month.
2.  **Apply complex grammatical structures** such as `أَنَّ` clauses, subjunctive verbs, light verb constructions, and correct noun/adjective/verb agreements.
3.  **Utilize stance markers and advanced connectors** to create logical flow, emphasize points, and show relationships between ideas.
4.  **Demonstrate morphological understanding** by correctly using verbs, Masdars, and participles derived from roots.
5.  **Pay meticulous attention to detail,** applying your newly acquired editing skills, especially regarding noun case endings, Idafa rules, and diptote behavior.

This is not about memorizing specific sentences, but about understanding the building blocks so well that you can construct new, sophisticated sentences on the fly. Let's practice bringing it all together!

## Examples

Here are some examples that combine many of the concepts you've learned this month. Try to identify as many grammatical structures and vocabulary categories as you can in each sentence!

1.  **"It is certain that the new government wants to undertake important negotiations for global peace. Moreover, the statistics show a big increase in diplomatic relations."**
    *   `مِنَ الْمُؤَكَّدِ أَنَّ الْحُكُومَةَ الْجَدِيدَةَ تُرِيدُ أَنْ تَقُومَ بِمُفَاوَضَاتٍ مُهِمَّةٍ لِلسَّلَامِ الْعَالَمِيِّ. عَلَاوَةً عَلَى ذَلِكَ، تُظْهِرُ الْإِحْصَاءَاتُ زِيَادَةً كَبِيرَةً فِي الْعَلَاقَاتِ الدِّبْلُومَاسِيَّةِ.`
    *   *Highlights:* Stance marker (`مِنَ الْمُؤَكَّدِ أَنَّ`), `أَنَّ` clause (Accusative subject `الْحُكُومَةَ`), adjective agreement (`الْجَدِيدَةَ`), light verb construction (`تَقُومَ بِـ`), Masdar `مُفَاوَضَاتٍ` (Genitive), Idafa (`السَّلَامِ الْعَالَمِيِّ`), advanced connector (`عَلَاوَةً عَلَى ذَلِكَ`), data description vocab (`تُظْهِرُ الْإِحْصَاءَاتُ زِيَادَةً`), non-human plural verb agreement (`تُظْهِرُ`), non-human plural adjective agreement (`الدِّبْلُومَاسِيَّةِ`).

2.  **"Despite the fact that the old conflict created a humanitarian crisis, the knowing judge believes that the protection of human rights is possible. And accordingly, he will submit a new report about justice."**
    *   `بِالرَّغْمِ مِنْ أَنَّ الصِّرَاعَ الْقَدِيمَ خَلَقَ أَزْمَةً إِنْسَانِيَّةً، يَعْتَقِدُ الْقَاضِي الْعَالِمُ أَنَّ حِفْظَ حُقُوقِ الْإِنْسَانِ مُمْكِنٌ. وَبِنَاءً عَلَى ذَلِكَ، سَيُقَدِّمُ تَقْرِيرًا جَدِيدًا عَنِ الْعَدَالَةِ.`
    *   *Highlights:* Advanced connector (`بِالرَّغْمِ مِنْ أَنَّ`), `أَنَّ` clause (Accusative subject `الصِّرَاعَ`), conflict vocab (`صِرَاعٌ`, `أَزْمَةٌ`), adjective agreement (`إِنْسَانِيَّةً`), morphology (active participle `الْعَالِمُ`), `أَنَّ` clause (`حِفْظَ حُقُوقِ الْإِنْسَانِ` - Idafa as subject), Masdar `حِفْظَ`, `مُمْكِنٌ` (possible), advanced connector (`وَبِنَاءً عَلَى ذَلِكَ`), verb `يُقَدِّمُ` (from Form II), data description vocab (`تَقْرِيرًا`), legal vocab (`الْعَدَالَةِ`).

3.  **"Unfortunately, the company faces difficult requirements for hiring new employees. On the other hand, the financial analysis shows a decrease in the stock market, especially since the current money is not enough."**
    *   `لِسُوءِ الْحَظِّ، تُوَاجِهُ الشَّرِكَةُ مُتَطَلَّبَاتٍ صَعْبَةً لِتَوْظِيفِ الْمُوَظَّفِينَ الْجُدُدِ. مِنْ جِهَةٍ أُخْرَى، يُظْهِرُ التَّحْلِيلُ الْمَالِيُّ نَقْصًا فِي سُوقِ الْأَسْهُمِ، خَاصَّةً وَأَنَّ الْمَالَ الْحَالِيَّ لَيْسَ كَافِيًا.`
    *   *Highlights:* Stance marker (`لِسُوءِ الْحَظِّ`), interview vocab (`الشَّرِكَةُ`, `مُتَطَلَّبَاتٍ`, `تَوْظِيفِ الْمُوَظَّفِينَ الْجُدُدِ`), advanced connector (`مِنْ جِهَةٍ أُخْرَى`), data description vocab (`يُظْهِرُ التَّحْلِيلُ الْمَالِيُّ نَقْصًا`), economic vocab (`سُوقِ الْأَسْهُمِ`), Idafa, advanced connector (`خَاصَّةً وَأَنَّ`), `أَنَّ` clause (Accusative subject `الْمَالَ`), `لَيْسَ كَافِيًا` (is not enough).

1.  **"It is known that the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ali, will present new proposals. In light of that, everyone believes that a diplomatic solution for the crisis is possible."**
    `مِنَ الْمَعْرُوفِ أَنَّ وَزِيرَ الْخَارِجِيَّةِ، عَلِيًّا، سَيُقَدِّمُ مُقْتَرَحَاتٍ جَدِيدَةً. فِي ضَوْءِ ذَلِكَ، يَعْتَقِدُ الْجَمِيعُ أَنَّ حَلًّا دِبْلُومَاسِيًّا لِلْأَزْمَةِ مُمْكِنٌ.`

    `بِالرَّغْمِ مِنْ أَنَّ الْعُنْفَ يَزْدَادُ فِي الدَّوْلَةِ، تُرِيدُ الْمَحْكَمَةُ أَنْ تَحْفَظَ حُقُوقَ الْإِنْسَانِ. وَفِي الْوَقْتِ نَفْسِهِ، تَبْحَثُ الشُّرْطَةُ عَنِ الْعَدَالَةِ لِلضَّحَايَا.`
