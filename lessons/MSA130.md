# Day 130 - Fluency: Science reporting

## Lesson

"Fluency: Science reporting" today is all about integrated application. You already possess a strong vocabulary for discussing science itself, scientific research, experiments, discoveries, results, developments, and the experts in these fields. The goal now is to use these terms within even more complex sentences, connecting scientific ideas to political, economic, environmental, or cultural contexts, and employing your full range of advanced connectors and stance markers.

Think of it as writing a sophisticated article or giving an oral report where you seamlessly blend scientific observations with analytical points, drawing connections between different aspects of a society.

To achieve this, remember to:

1.  **Recall `Science Reporting` Vocabulary (Day 123):**
    *   `عِلْمِيٌّ` / `عِلْمِيَّةٌ` (scientific)
    *   `أَبْحَاثٌ` (research projects, studies)
    *   `تَجْرِبَةٌ` / `تَجَارِبُ` (experiment / experiments)
    *   `اكْتِشَافٌ` / `اكْتِشَافَاتٌ` (discovery / discoveries)
    *   `يَكْتَشِفُ` (he discovers)
    *   `نَتِيجَةٌ` / `نَتَائِجُ` (result / results)
    *   `تَطَوُّرٌ` (development) / `يَتَطَوَّرُ` (he develops)
    *   `خَبِيرٌ` / `خُبَرَاءُ` (expert / experts)
    *   `يُثْبِتُ` (he proves, establishes)
    *   `يُشِيرُ إِلَى` (he indicates, points to)
    *   `الطِّبُّ` (medicine)
    *   `الْعُلَمَاءُ` (scholars)

2.  **Integrate `Argumentation Structures` (Day 125):** Use phrases to state claims, add support, present counter-arguments, and draw conclusions.
    *   `مِنَ الْوَاضِحِ أَنَّ` (It is clear that...)
    *   `يَعْتَقِدُ ... أَنَّ` (... believes that...)
    *   `لَا شَكَّ أَنَّ` (There is no doubt that...)
    *   `مِنَ الْمَعْرُوفِ أَنَّ` (It is known that...)
    *   `لِسُوءِ الْحَظِّ` (Unfortunately...)
    *   `مِنَ الْغَرِيبِ أَنَّ` (It is strange that...)

3.  **Utilize `Advanced Connectors` (Days 107-108):** Ensure smooth transitions and logical relationships between ideas.
    *   `عَلَاوَةً عَلَى ذَلِكَ` (Moreover...)
    *   `مِنْ جِهَةٍ أُخْرَى` (On the other hand...)
    *   `بِالرَّغْمِ مِنْ أَنَّ` (Despite the fact that...)
    *   `فِي ضَوْءِ ذَلِكَ` (In light of that...)
    *   `وَبِنَاءً عَلَى ذَلِكَ` (And accordingly...)
    *   `خَاصَّةً وَأَنَّ` (Especially since...)
    *   `وَفِي الْوَقْتِ نَفْسِهِ` (At the same time...)

4.  **Maintain `Grammatical Accuracy` (Days 118-119):** Pay meticulous attention to noun case endings, adjective agreement (especially with non-human plurals), verb-subject agreement, Idafa constructions, `أَنَّ` vs `أَنْ`, and diptote behavior.

This lesson will help you articulate nuanced and sophisticated insights about science, linking it to various aspects of society and demonstrating a high level of Arabic proficiency.

## Examples

Let's look at some examples that weave together science reporting with other learned concepts. Try to identify the various structures and vocabulary in each sentence!

1.  **"It is clear that scientific development is important for the future of the state. Moreover, new research indicates significant discoveries in the field of medicine."**
    *   `مِنَ الْوَاضِحِ أَنَّ التَّطَوُّرَ الْعِلْمِيَّ مُهِمٌّ لِمُسْتَقْبَلِ الدَّوْلَةِ. عَلَاوَةً عَلَى ذَلِكَ، تُشِيرُ الْأَبْحَاثُ الْجَدِيدَةُ إِلَى اكْتِشَافَاتٍ مُهِمَّةٍ فِي مَجَالِ الطِّبِّ.`
    *   **Breakdown:**
        *   `مِنَ الْوَاضِحِ أَنَّ` (It is clear that - Stance marker, Day 125).
        *   `التَّطَوُّرَ الْعِلْمِيَّ مُهِمٌّ لِمُسْتَقْبَلِ الدَّوْلَةِ` (scientific development is important for the future of the state - `التَّطَوُّرَ الْعِلْمِيَّ` (development, scientific, Day 123) Accusative subject of `أَنَّ`; `مُهِمٌّ` (important, Day 54) Nominative predicate; `لِمُسْتَقْبَلِ الدَّوْلَةِ` (for the future of the state, Day 103/101)).
        *   `عَلَاوَةً عَلَى ذَلِكَ` (Moreover - Advanced connector, Day 107).
        *   `تُشِيرُ الْأَبْحَاثُ الْجَدِيدَةُ إِلَى اكْتِشَافَاتٍ مُهِمَّةٍ فِي مَجَالِ الطِّبِّ` (new research indicates important discoveries in the field of medicine - `تُشِيرُ الْأَبْحَاثُ الْجَدِيدَةُ إِلَى` (indicates new research, Day 123/54); `اكْتِشَافَاتٍ مُهِمَّةٍ` (important discoveries, Day 123/54) Genitive after `إِلَى`; `فِي مَجَالِ الطِّبِّ` (in the field of medicine, Day 116/123)).

2.  **"Despite the fact that the experiments are difficult, the scholars are undertaking many of them. In light of that, everyone believes that the results will be positive."**
    *   `بِالرَّغْمِ مِنْ أَنَّ التَّجَارِبَ صَعْبَةٌ، يَقُومُ الْعُلَمَاءُ بِالْكَثِيرِ مِنْهَا. فِي ضَوْءِ ذَلِكَ، يَعْتَقِدُ الْجَمِيعُ أَنَّ النَّتَائِجَ سَتَكُونُ إِيجَابِيَّةً.`
    *   **Breakdown:**
        *   `بِالرَّغْمِ مِنْ أَنَّ` (Despite the fact that - Advanced connector, Day 107).
        *   `التَّجَارِبَ صَعْبَةٌ` (the experiments are difficult - `التَّجَارِبَ` (experiments, Day 123) Accusative subject of `أَنَّ`; `صَعْبَةٌ` (difficult, Day 27)).
        *   `يَقُومُ الْعُلَمَاءُ بِالْكَثِيرِ مِنْهَا` (the scholars are undertaking many of them - `يَقُومُ بِـ` (undertake, Day 97); `الْعُلَمَاءُ` (scholars, Day 123) Nominative subject; `الْكَثِيرِ مِنْهَا` (many of them, Day 110/24)).
        *   `فِي ضَوْءِ ذَلِكَ` (In light of that - Advanced connector, Day 108).
        *   `يَعْتَقِدُ الْجَمِيعُ أَنَّ النَّتَائِجَ سَتَكُونُ إِيجَابِيَّةً` (everyone believes that the results will be positive - `يَعْتَقِدُ الْجَمِيعُ أَنَّ` (Day 79/110); `النَّتَائِجَ` (results, Day 123) Accusative subject of `أَنَّ`; `سَتَكُونُ إِيجَابِيَّةً` (will be positive, Day 98/113)).

3.  **"Unfortunately, the scientific report shows a big decrease in funding for research. On the other hand, the experts believe that pollution affects human health."**
    *   `لِسُوءِ الْحَظِّ، يُظْهِرُ التَّقْرِيرُ الْعِلْمِيُّ نَقْصًا كَبِيرًا فِي تَمْوِيلِ الْأَبْحَاثِ. مِنْ جِهَةٍ أُخْرَى، يَعْتَقِدُ الْخُبَرَاءُ أَنَّ التَّلَوُّثَ يُؤَثِّرُ فِي صِحَّةِ الْإِنْسَانِ.`
    *   **Breakdown:**
        *   `لِسُوءِ الْحَظِّ` (Unfortunately - Stance marker, Day 99).
        *   `يُظْهِرُ التَّقْرِيرُ الْعِلْمِيُّ نَقْصًا كَبِيرًا فِي تَمْوِيلِ الْأَبْحَاثِ` (the scientific report shows a big decrease in funding for research - `يُظْهِرُ التَّقْرِيرُ الْعِلْمِيُّ` (shows the scientific report, Day 115/123); `نَقْصًا كَبِيرًا` (big decrease, Day 115/54) Accusative direct object; `فِي تَمْوِيلِ الْأَبْحَاثِ` (in funding for research - `تَمْوِيلٌ` (funding, new for this context) Genitive after `فِي`, first term of Idafa; `الْأَبْحَاثِ` (research, Day 123) Genitive).
        *   `مِنْ جِهَةٍ أُخْرَى` (On the other hand - Advanced connector, Day 108).
        *   `يَعْتَقِدُ الْخُبَرَاءُ أَنَّ التَّلَوُّثَ يُؤَثِّرُ فِي صِحَّةِ الْإِنْسَانِ` (the experts believe that pollution affects human health - `يَعْتَقِدُ الْخُبَرَاءُ أَنَّ` (Day 123/79); `التَّلَوُّثَ` (pollution, Day 124) Accusative subject of `أَنَّ`; `يُؤَثِّرُ فِي` (affects, Day 122); `صِحَّةِ الْإِنْسَانِ` (human health - `صِحَّةٌ` (health, Day 126) Genitive after `فِي`, first term of Idafa; `الْإِنْسَانِ` (human, Day 106) Genitive).

## 📚 Core Vocabulary
*   تَمْوِيلٌ (tamweelun) - funding (masculine Masdar/noun)
*   تِقْنِيَّاتٌ حَدِيثَةٌ (tiqniyyātun ḥadeethatun) - modern technologies (feminine plural noun + adjective)

*   يَعْتَمِدُ عَلَى (ya'tamidu 'alā) - he depends on (present tense verb + preposition)
